Poem #1

Potential

It started with no stress,

And no pressure

But by result there was no potential

As time went on

Weight added a ton

And it was ready to go celestial

When the weight goes away

The spring will shoot straight up

The reason I chose this poem is because I liked the metaphor that I put in place. I liked the idea of my current position being a compressed spring and eventually I will be let free.

Poem #2

Net Neutrality

The internet was meant to be decentralized
to a point
Nobody should be limited
There was no reason for change
The petitioner is a thorn
Putting cuffs on typing hands

Nothing needed to change, yet a thorn
in everyone's side decided it would be a good idea
One little vote and many fake petitions
got the ball rolling
a ball that no one could stop
because the thorn kept pushing

all the real grievances were drowned
since there are more fake people
then there are real

I chose this poem because it was about something that I cared about. I felt that since I cared about it then it would make a good addition to the porfolio.

Poem #3

Moving Memories

Goodbye old world,
Old friends,
Now you are distant memories,
There is only this green car
And endless road ahead

Midway through,
We take a pitstop.
There are walls of water crashing down
And mist that dampens the air

Eventually we leave this world,
As well.
The final destination,
That’s all there’s left.
With an old green car,
And a few souvenirs,
We enter the new world
That I seem to have always known.

While this poem seems darker than it really is, it is just one of my earliest memories. I chose this because it is a reminder of some of my earliest memories, moving to Massachusetts.

Grades need to be good and fun is the last thing on my mind. The entire world to a six-year-old doesn't contain stress the only things that exist are enjoyment and boredom. The entirety of my world when I was six revolved around turning boredom into enjoyment. When there was nothing to do boredom slowly crept up to take over the world. WNB #3

Well, today is the day. The entire family was gathered to hear Aunt Edna's will. I hope I will get at least something that would alleviate the money struggles that I have sort of been dealing with. At this point in time, I had no idea what was in store for me. Everyone was called into what sort of looked like a boardroom but not really. The lawyer sat at the front welcoming us. He then asked us to quiet down and he began. "Before we begin", he said, "This is the finalized will of Edna Smith. Everything written in this will is final and cannot be changed." He then continued with all of the legal mumbo-jumbo. Then he paused. He looked somewhat confused. As if he had not seen this before. He looked up and promptly asked. "Before I go any further who is the youngest person present above or at the age of 18?" Everyone immediately looked at me. I then raised my hand and he asked for my name. I responded hesitantly, "Adam is my name sir". He then asked me to stand up and make my way up to him. Once I made it up to him he asked me to wait for him to finish reading the will. He picked back up saying "I Edna Smith hereby leave all my worldly possessions that being: property, savings, investments and all items of value once owned by me to..." he paused once again and looked at me. He then continued "to Adam Smith" he finally finished. He closed the folder and explained to everyone it asked for the youngest person at or above the age of 18. I was shocked. I had just become magnitudes richer. The lawyer saw me in shock and sat down once again. He opened his folder and began reading again, "The inheritance to the person named can and will only proceed if said person reveals at this time their deepest darkest secret to everyone present in the room. If this condition is not met all possessions in question are to be donated to charity or sold and have the profits donated to charity." He stopped and my heart almost sank. I was gonna throw up. I was so close to being rich yet so dar. I couldn't possibly think about sharing this secret, not in front of my parents and the rest of the family. I had only ever told aunt Edna. But this couldn't have been done to make me share my secret could it? I was being torn up from the inside out. I couldn't let all this wealth slip from my hands. I had made my decision I was gonna come clean. I opened my mouth "..." nothing came out. I tried again "Ah... umm... My..." I just couldn't do it. Once again I tried, "My-My biggest sec-secret that I've never told anyone... is..."....".
